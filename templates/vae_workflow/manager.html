How It Works

  1. 1 Add section editors to the VAE pool using the search on the left.
  2. 2 When an article enters the VAE Claiming stage, pool members can browse and claim articles they wish to handle.
  3. 3 The handling editor reviews all claims, confirms one, and the VAE is assigned as section editor.
  4. 4 On revision, the article is fast-tracked back to the same VAE.
